The Story of Lili:

A History of the Magnanimous Corgi

Lili was born in rural Oregon in a haven full of dozens of Corgis. When she was 8 weeks old she left her home to go live with a new family.
This family loves her dearly despite her phobia of large circles and other dogs. She enjoys activities such as watching TV,
stealing your food, and riding the family cat. She is a mere five months old so much of her story has yet to be told.

Some of Lili's Favorite Things

  • Food (family's food!)
  • Chasing her ball
  • Carrying socks around like puppies
  • Making nests from toilet paper
  • Taking long naps
  • Puppering around
